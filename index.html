Connected Car Hackathon in Berlin.

About the hackathon

You are Designer or Android / iOS developer and love cars?

Then you should join us October 18-19th in Berlin because together with Bosch and Bosch SoftTec experts we will host a hackathon around „mySPIN“ (Thats a smartphone integration for cars like the Range Rover Evoque). We will have an awesome jury and great prizes for the best car app or app adaption for the car - that means you can also bring your existing app and use the SDK to enhance it.

If you are interested shoot us an email at carhack@hack.institute for further information.
